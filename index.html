How HandAuth Pro Works

A structured four-step screening process. Designed to integrate as the first gate in any document verification workflow — for individuals, lawyers, or automated pipelines.

01

Submit Document

Upload a signed document (PDF or image scan) plus reference signatures. No files leave your environment — processing is local.

02

AI Extraction

The engine extracts stroke structure, letter formation, proportions, pen-lift points, spacing, and baseline alignment from the submitted signature.

03

Comparative Analysis

Advanced algorithms compare structural patterns against reference signatures, identifying anomalies and inconsistencies that suggest potential forgery.

04

Risk Report

Receive a structured risk score and anomaly report. Low-risk: proceed. High-risk: escalate to a forensic document examiner.

HandAuth Pro covers steps 1–3. Step 4 — engaging a certified forensic document examiner — is always your decision and strongly recommended for legally significant matters.

What HandAuth Pro Detects

Four core screening capabilities — each designed to surface specific fraud signals without requiring a live forensic expert for every submission.

Risk Probability Score

An orientational score (Low / Medium / High risk) indicating whether the signature shows early warning signs — not a definitive expert verdict.

Non-Handwritten Artifact Detection

Flags printed text overlays, screen captures, and likely synthetic (AI-generated) signatures — basic red flags that eliminate obvious counterfeits fast.

Structural Comparison Report

Visual summary of structural similarities and deviations between the disputed signature and your reference samples — for internal triage use.

Multiple Reference Support

Upload several known-genuine reference signatures to improve comparison accuracy and reduce false positives in the triage output.

Note: All outputs are indicative and intended for internal triage decisions — supporting expert review, not replacing it.
